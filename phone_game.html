Phone Game
Language used
 ActionScript 3.0 was used.

What it is
 This is an educational Super Mario style phone app I lead the development of as part of a group of three. We were tasked with developing a game for a group of third grade children.


The game. An unlock all levels option and keyboard controls have been added.
Move left: A or left arrow
Move right: D or right arrow
Jump: W, up arrow or space bar

My Role
 I was mainly responsible for the creation of a collision engine, special interactive objects like springs and moving platforms as well as a cookie based save system to remember the unlocked levels. I was the only one in the group who had already used ActionScript 3.0 on my own outside of school, so I also helped teach my partners and reviewed their code.
